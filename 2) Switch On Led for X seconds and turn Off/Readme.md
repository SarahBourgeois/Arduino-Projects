# :bulb: Switch on LED for X seconds or minutes and turn Off :bulb:

This code allow you to switch on a led when you press a button. The LED is High during the time you want and turn off.

### Sketch : 

- 2 resistors  220ohm
- 1 push button
- 1 LED
- wires
- 1 breadboard 
- 1 Arduino
